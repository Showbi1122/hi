People hire me because I answer within 24 hours, ship SEO-ready builds, and stay accountable after launch. No account managers, no surprise invoices.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions I get before a project starts.

Who are you?

Malik Taleeb Shahbaz, web developer and software engineer based in Pakistan, working with clients in the US, UK, UAE, Australia, Europe, and elsewhere. I build custom sites, SaaS apps, and SEO-focused web products.

What do you actually build?

Custom websites, SaaS MVPs, React/Next.js frontends, landing pages, lead-gen sites, and builds with SEO fundamentals included. Stack depends on the project, we pick what fits, not what's trendy.

Do you work with clients outside Pakistan?

Yes, that's most of my work. US, Canada, UK, Australia, Germany, UAE, Saudi Arabia, and more. See location pages for how I handle time zones and communication in each market.

React or Next.js, which do you use?

Both. React for logged-in apps and dashboards where SEO doesn't matter much. Next.js for marketing sites and anything that needs to rank on Google. I wrote a longer comparison if you want the full breakdown.

Do you handle SEO?

Yes, semantic HTML, meta tags, schema markup, sitemaps, fast load times, and content structure that works for Google and AI answer tools. I don't promise page-one rankings in week one; I build the foundation properly.

How do we start?

WhatsApp or LinkedIn works best. Tell me what you're trying to build. I'll reply within a day with follow-up questions and a rough quote.

What's your stack?

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, React, Next.js, Vue.js, Node.js, Firebase, Bootstrap, Tailwind, deployed on Vercel unless you need something else. I match tools to the project.

Why hire you instead of an agency?

You get one person who writes the code and answers your messages. I've shipped sites at Nexelix and 247Marketers; now I take on freelance work where I can move fast and stay accountable directly to the client.
